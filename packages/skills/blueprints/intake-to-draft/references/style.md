# Style

<!-- guide: The rules the assistant applies on every draft — keep them few and concrete. Replace them with your house style. -->

- Voice: formal, firm, courteous. No rhetorical questions; no threats beyond the steps announced in the interview.
- Length: one page.
- Dates written out in words ("10 June 2026") — numeric dates read differently across jurisdictions.
- Amounts with the currency code, and in words once: "EUR 12 400 (twelve thousand four hundred euro)".
- Unknowns stay `[TO CONFIRM]`; never invent names, dates, or amounts.
