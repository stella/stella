# M&A Regulatory Filing

Steps for regulatory filing identification:
1. Determine transaction value and industry
2. Check merger control thresholds (jurisdiction-specific)
3. Identify FDI screening requirements
4. Flag sector-specific regulatory approvals (banking, telecom, defence, etc.)
5. Estimate filing timelines and prepare documentation
