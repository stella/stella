# Legal Citation Format

Ensure citations follow the correct format for the target jurisdiction:

- **US (Bluebook):** `Party v. Party, Volume Reporter Page (Court Year)`
- **UK (OSCOLA):** `Case Name [Year] Volume Report Page (Court)`
- **EU (ECLI):** `ECLI:XX:YYYY:C:NNN`
- **Canada (McGill):** `Party v Party, [Year] Volume Reporter (Jurisdiction) Page`
- **Australia (AGLC):** `Party v Party (Year) Volume Reporter, Page (Court)`

When uncertain about jurisdiction, default to the jurisdiction of the query or ask the user.
