# Table improvements

## Working with larger tables is faster, clearer, and easier to follow

Thank you for the feedback on the initial release.

Based on your comments, we focused on making the table experience better and added the following improvements:

- You can now flag a call as reviewed or requiring follow-up, with a visual indicator directly in the table.
- Tables now render with full-screen rows, making the workspace feel more spacious.
- You can expand individual cells, or expand all cells at once to fit their content.

More improvements are coming soon. Please keep the feedback coming.
